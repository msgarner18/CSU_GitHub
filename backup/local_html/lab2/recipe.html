Do you like chicken?! You'll LOVE these chicken enchiladas!

Ingredients:

10 tortillas   1 can of chicken
1 cup of salsa   1 cup of cheese
1 cup of sour cream   1 onion
1 oz. of salt   1 oz. of pepper

Instructions:

Filling

  1. dice the onion and add it to a pan with the chicken
  2. cook the onions and chicken to taste. Be sure to add salt and pepper

Assembly

  1. add filling to tortillas and layer them in a pan.
  2. sprinkle the cheese over top of the enchiladas
  3. cook for 3 hours on 100 degrees fehrenheight

Serve

let enchiladas cool and then lather the enchilada in salsa and sour cream and place enchilada in mouth for optimal enjoyment of the enchilada. Enjoy!
